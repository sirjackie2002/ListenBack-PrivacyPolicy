Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ListenBack! This privacy policy details how ListenBack, developed by Novurae, handles information and respects your privacy. Using our app confirms your agreement to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Process

ListenBack operates entirely offline and does not transmit or store personal data on any external servers. Here are the types of information the app processes locally on your device:

  • Audio Recordings: Created and managed by you; stored only on your device.
  • Location Data: Used for setting location-based alarms. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Alarm Settings: Configurations for alarms are handled by your device and never transmitted outside.

Permissions Required

To perform its functions, ListenBack requires several permissions. These permissions are necessary for accessing device functionalities but do not allow the app to gather data for external use:

  • Audio Recording: To create audio clips as per user commands.
  • Location Access: To enable functionality of location-based alarms.
  • Storage Access: To save and manage recordings and alarm configurations locally.
  • Exact Alarm Scheduling: To schedule alarms that trigger at precise times.

Your Rights and Controls

As ListenBack does not collect or store personal data, there are no direct means to request data access or deletion. All data you create and manage through the app resides solely on your device, ensuring you maintain complete control over your personal information and its security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
